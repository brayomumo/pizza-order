PIZZA HOUSE

A pizza House pizza box might be one of the most recognisable icons of Africa. Whichever way you slice it, pizza House is an industry leader in the quick service pizza game. The first pizza House outlet opened its doors in 1994 in Harare, Zimbabwe, and rose quickly in popularity. Serving hot oven-fresh pizza with its signature all-natural tomato sauce and using only the freshest ingredients, pizza House has established itself at the top of the industry and is easily one of the first choices in quick service pizza in Africa.
